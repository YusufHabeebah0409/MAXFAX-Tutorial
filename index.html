Purpose of the Tutorial

The primary purpose of this tutorial program is to provide students with additional academic support outside of regular classes. By engaging in group discussions, taking online tests, and accessing Q&A sessions, students are better equipped to handle exam-related pressure and develop a deeper understanding of the course materials. This platform ensures that students can enhance their learning at their own pace.
